# Print your own Professional Bicyle Cards

https://youtu.be/hBcDbpYeSUE

1. Remove finish with Acetone and a soft towel.
2. Template: page of paper with cutout the size of the card and double-stick tape to hold the card in place within the page on all 4 sides
3. Print onto Template
4. re-seal with spray fixitive/new finish "Krylon Workable Fixative" recommended.
5. tape cards printed-side up in brown paper bag, spray with fixative, use hair drier to remove excess fumes, remove cards and dry with hair drier.
